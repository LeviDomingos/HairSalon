About Us

Welcome to Shamarie Mobile & Salon, we are an afro-centric hairdressing business herein our primary focus is to deliver exquisite afro influence hairstyle to those who desire it. We have a small team of highly skilled and trained workforce who carries a vast wealth of hairdressing knowledge more so for individuals with an African heritage.

Shamarie Mobile & Salon has been growing from strength to strength for the past 7 years although the founder and chief stylist Shashia Thomas has been professionally working in the afro hairdressing business for more than 10 years.

What you can expect from Shamarie Mobile & Salon is professionalism where each and every client is treated with the utmost respect regardless of sex, age or gender: WE DON'T DISCRIMINATE. Also, we appreciate that clients want to get in and get out as soon as possible therefore all booked appointments will take priority over unscheduled visits.
